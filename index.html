Summary

I am a prospective graduate student pursuing a PhD in the Computer Science field. Interests include robotics and digital security. I have been a teaching assistant since August 2022 for the introductory C/C++ course at Washington State University. Career goal is to become a career-track professor.

From August 2023 to May 2024, I worked on my capstone project for my major. Our group developed an app to assist mute child patients express essential needs and desires. The app runs on Android devices, built using Flutter and Dart. The user interface consists of squares - each containing an icon and associated color. I worked on the backend and did not work extensively with the user interface. I focused on tile creation, database implementation, and database configuration. A factory pattern was used for tile creation.

I have knowledge of C++, C, C#, Swift, Python, ARM9 assembly, Verilog, and exposure to many other languages. I created a video for students learning how to use git in 300-level courses. The video covers simple setup, explains when to commit, and proper usage of branches.
